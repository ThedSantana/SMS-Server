How to use SMS Server

Step 1: Connect your devices to Wi-Fi

Connect your phone and computer to the same Wi-Fi network. Wi-Fi is necessary so that your phone can communicate with your computer. SMS Server works just like a regular website, but it's hosted by your phone.

Step 2: Activate SMS Server

Flip the switch at the top of the settings screen to start the server. Before you do, feel free to configure your security settings. The default password is “alpine” (without the quotes).

Step 3: Log into the Website

If you have manual authentication off, enter your password in the text box. The default password is “alpine”. You can change the password in the app by If manual authentication is on, a notification will appear on your device. Click the notification to allow that computer access to SMS Server.
